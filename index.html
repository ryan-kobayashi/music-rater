Frequently Asked Questions

How does the rating system work on this website?

For every song, users can rate the song from one to five stars. They can also attach a review to the song to further customize their review!

Why use this app as opposed to another music rating app on the market?

With our app, you can rate music, customize your profile, and create custom playlists depending on your favorite songs! This is a music rating app but it also can double as a social music sharing app as well.

Where do I start?

Starting is super easy! Just hit the sign up button in the top corner and rate away!
